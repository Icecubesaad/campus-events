Popular Destinations

Discover the most beautiful places in Pakistan

Hunza Valley

Location: Gilgit-Baltistan

A stunning valley surrounded by snow-capped peaks, known for its breathtaking beauty, apricot blossoms, and warm hospitality.

Best Time: April to October

Skardu

Location: Gilgit-Baltistan

Gateway to some of the world's highest peaks including K2. Home to beautiful lakes like Shangrila and Satpara.

Best Time: May to September

Swat Valley

Location: Khyber Pakhtunkhwa

Known as the "Switzerland of Pakistan," featuring lush green meadows, clear rivers, and ancient Buddhist heritage sites.

Best Time: March to October

Lahore

Location: Punjab

The cultural heart of Pakistan with magnificent Mughal architecture, vibrant food scene, and rich historical landmarks.

Best Time: October to March

Fairy Meadows

Location: Gilgit-Baltistan

A grassland near Nanga Parbat base camp, offering spectacular views of the ninth highest mountain in the world.

Best Time: June to September
